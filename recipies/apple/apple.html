Honeyed Apple

back to index

350F (175C)

  • 4 crisp apples
  • 120ml (1/2cp) honey, + more to drizzle
  1. Preheat oven to 175C (350F)
  2. Use a melon baller or small strong spoon to scoop out the cores from above. Leave the bottom of the apple alone so that your "honeypot" doesn't leak while baking.
  3. Fill your apple's "honeypot" with 30 ml (2 TBSP, or 1/8cp) of honey.
  4. Bake 15 min at 175C (350F), until tender
  5. Plate to serve. Drizzle more honey if you wish. You could even add some spices such as cinnamon. It's ready!
