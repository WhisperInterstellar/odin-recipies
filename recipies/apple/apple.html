Honeyed Apple

♡back to index

Description

If you've never had this, you need to have this. If you need to use a different sweetener, either choose a similarly syrupy one or combine a dry one with enough of something buttery to make a little ball of "paste" or "dough". Try finishing this sweet and simple treat with a little spice on top, such as cinnamon or powdered ginger.

Ingredients

  • 4 crisp apples
  • 120ml (1/2cp) honey, + more to drizzle

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F)
  2. Use a melon baller or small strong spoon to scoop out the cores from above. Leave the bottom of the apple alone so that your "honeypot" doesn't leak while baking.
  3. Fill your apple's "honeypot" with 30 ml (2 TBSP, or 1/8cp) of honey each.
  4. Bake 15 min at 175°C (350°F), until tender.
  5. Plate to serve. Drizzle more honey if you wish.

It's ready to eat!
