Description

A great way to use up scraps from other recipies like broccoli stems or part of a carrot. Just avoid oil or any excess moisture, as it will keep the rice from forming a shape.

Ingredients

  • 4 servings steamed short grain rice (240ml/1cp dry, see link above)
  • 240ml (1cp) "Any Vegetable"

Steps

  1. Any measuring/cuttiing/pre-cooking. Vegetables should be broken down to pea-sized morsels and tender enough to bite easily.
  2. Be sure everything is cool enough to handle, fan/pat dry for best rice adhesion.
  3. Mix rice and veg thouroughly. Use moistened clean hands to press into fun shapes such as triangles.
  4. OPTIONAL: apply an edible wrap to help keep rice off hands while eating. Try such options as nori (seaweed) or tortillas (not the hard fried kind) or even lettuce. It's yours to create and enjoy!

It's ready to eat!
