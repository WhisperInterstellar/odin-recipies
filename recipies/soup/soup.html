Cream of Mushroom Soup

back to index

Blurb later

  • 16 oz mushrooms (variety) + enough oil/butter to saute
  • 2 tsp "any herb" (I like equal parts Parsley Sage Rosemary and Thyme)
  • 12 oz water
  • 8 oz sour cream + OPTIONAL flour to thicken
  • Salt to taste

  1. Prep by de-stemming and mincing heerbs as nescesary and chopping mushrooms as you like them
  2. In a pot coat bottom with oil and saute herbs and mushrooms 5-10 min, until browned and aromatic
  3. Add salt and cover with 300ml (1.5cp) of water. Cover.
  4. Simmer over low heat for 30 min
  5. OPTIONAL for thicker soup, mix flour and enough additional water to moisten, then add to the sour cream
  6. Add dairy (or dairy mixture) in last few minutes of cooking (5 or less)
  7. Remove from heat, cool somewhat before serving/storing.

It's ready to eat!
