Cream of Mushroom Soup

back to index

Description

This is definitely not like the condensed canned stuff. It's much better! Great with a side of garlic bread. Chose whatever mushrooms and herbs are available in your area and appealing to you. Use fresh if you can get it for a richer flavor. Non-dairy substitutions are completely delicious, if you so chose!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz mushrooms (variety) + enough oil/butter to saute
  • 2 tsp "any herb" (I like Parsley, Sage, Rosemary, and Thyme)
  • 12 oz water
  • 8 oz sour cream + OPTIONAL flour to thicken
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prep by de-stemming and mincing heerbs as nescesary and chopping mushrooms as you like them. If any of your ingredients are dried, now would be the time to re-hydrate them for a few minutes to improve flavor and texture.
  2. In a pot coat bottom with oil and saute herbs and mushrooms 5-10 min, until browned and aromatic.
  3. Add salt and cover with 300ml (1.5cp) of water. If you are unsure about the quantity of salt, test now with a clean spoon and adjust to any preferences or health needs. Cover with an apropriately sized lid.
  4. Simmer over low heat for 30 min.
  5. OPTIONAL for thicker soup, mix a few tbsp flour and water until moist and a little runny. Then add that to the sour cream.
  6. Add dairy (or dairy mixture) in last few minutes of cooking (5 or less)
  7. Remove from heat. Once it is no longer scalding hot, serve or store.

It's ready to eat!
